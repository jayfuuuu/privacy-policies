Privacy Policy

Life Grid – Mobile Application

Life Grid ("we", "our", or "the app") is developed by Jay Fu. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Life Grid mobile application.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

Life Grid stores the following data that you enter:

  • Birthday and life expectancy settings
  • Selected life themes
  • Weekly records including tasks, events, habits, reflections, mood, and memories

All of this data is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal content on any server.

Subscription Data

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. When you subscribe, RevenueCat processes the transaction through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. RevenueCat may collect:

  • Anonymous app user ID
  • Purchase history and subscription status
  • Device identifiers for transaction validation

For details, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or tracking tools
  • We do not serve advertisements
  • We do not share or sell any data to third parties

Data Storage & Security

All your content (weekly records, tasks, events, reflections) is stored locally on your device using encrypted local storage. We have no access to this data. If you delete the app, all local data will be permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

Life Grid does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
